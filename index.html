Quick Facts & Character Traits

Planning

In a game of chess, the best players are able to plan 5 steps ahead of their opponents. They consider all of the possibilites and take the best choice, factoring in the risk, return, and other counter measures. Similarly, I create a realistic and organized plan, keeping me on the right track towards accomplishment.

Leadership

On the job and off the job, I always strive to be a great leader to others. I strive to keep strong communication, exemplify diligence, motivate teammates, give my best input, and attentively listen to others' inputs. In every situation, I strive to be a role model and a team player, to make progress and move closer towards the goal.

Passionate

I am always passionate about what I do. Being passionate takes away the feeling of doing something like it's a chore, but to doing something because you enjoy it. If you love what you do, it shows, and you spread positivity to others.

Fun

When it's cruch time, it's crunch time. But that doesn't mean there's no room left for fun when the context is appropriate. I always like to keep a balance of fun - a happy and motivated individual will produce far better results and contribute to a healthy relationship with their teammates, clients, and friends.
